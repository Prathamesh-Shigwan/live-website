Our Process

From Pollution
To Production

01
🗑️
Collection
We partner with municipalities and waste collectors to source post-consumer plastic from oceans, streets, and landfills.
02
🔬
Sorting & Analysis
AI-powered optical sorting classifies plastic by type with 99.7% accuracy before processing.
03
⚙️
Processing
Plastics are shredded, washed, melted, and pelletized into ultra-pure recycled resin ready for manufacturing.
04
🏭
Manufacturing
Our solar-powered facilities mold recycled pellets into premium products meeting virgin plastic standards.
05
✅
Quality Control
Every product passes rigorous stress, chemical, and UV-resistance testing before leaving our facility.
06
🌍
Distribution
Carbon-neutral logistics delivers your eco-friendly products worldwide with full chain-of-custody traceability.
