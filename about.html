We Are hitshi

Born from a vision to end plastic pollution — by turning it into something beautiful and useful.

Our Story

We Started With
One Question

What if the 380 million tons of plastic produced every year wasn't a problem — but a resource?

Founded in 2018 by environmental engineer Dr. Leila Hassan and materials scientist Prof. Nkosi Okafor, hitshi set out to prove that recycled plastics can equal — and even surpass — virgin materials in quality and performance.

Today we operate three manufacturing facilities across Europe, Asia, and the Americas, processing over 12 tons of post-consumer plastic daily and distributing 340+ product lines to 60+ global partners.

Our Journey

Built Year by Year

2018

hitshi Founded

Dr. Leila Hassan and Prof. Nkosi Okafor launch hitshi in Rotterdam with a pilot plant processing 200kg/day of rPET.

2019

First Product Line

EcoSit Garden Chair launches to critical acclaim, winning the European Green Product Award. 50,000 units sold in 8 months.

2020

Scale-Up & Series A

€12M Series A funding secured. Capacity expanded to 5 tons/day. RecyDeck Lumber line launched for construction sector.

2021

Ocean Plastic Program

Partnership with 14 coastal communities launched, targeting ocean and shoreline plastic recovery across Southeast Asia.

2022

Asia-Pacific Expansion

Second manufacturing facility opens in Singapore. Processing capacity reaches 8 tons/day. ISO 14001 certification achieved.

2023

Americas Launch

Third facility opens in São Paulo, Brazil. North and South American distribution network established with 20+ new partners.

2024

Carbon Neutral Achieved

100% renewable energy across all facilities. Carbon-neutral certification from Bureau Veritas. 340+ active product SKUs.

2026

Today — 12 Tons/Day

12 tons processed daily, 60+ global partners, 4.2M kg of ocean plastic recovered. And we're just getting started.
